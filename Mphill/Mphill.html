MPHILL PROGRAMMES

Diverse courses cover software development in-depth.


Research-intensive, specialized studies advancing computer science knowledge and innovation.


Foundational courses in algorithms, programming, and computer systems for undergraduates.


In-depth studies merging theory, research, and practical skills in computing

The MPhil in Computer Science provides advanced training in cutting-edge fields. Students learn about research methods, algorithms, and data science. The emphasis on innovation sharpens machine learning and artificial intelligence skills. Scholars gain a thorough understanding of computer science principles through rigorous coursework. The programme encourages critical thinking, problem solving, and collaboration, preparing graduates to make significant contributions to academia or industry. Customizable tracks cater to a wide range of interests, ensuring a well-rounded education. MPhil candidates conduct scholarly research, pushing the boundaries of knowledge in a rapidly changing field.
