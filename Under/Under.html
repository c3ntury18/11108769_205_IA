UNDERGRADUATE PROGRAMMES

Diverse courses cover software development in-depth.


Research-intensive, specialized studies advancing computer science knowledge and innovation.


Foundational courses in algorithms, programming, and computer systems for undergraduates.


In-depth studies merging theory, research, and practical skills in computing

TThe University of Ghana's Computer Science Department offers comprehensive undergraduate programs, encompassing courses such as Data Structures, Algorithms, Artificial Intelligence, Software Engineering, and Computer Networks. These programs equip students with a robust understanding of theoretical concepts and practical skills. Through hands-on projects and cutting-edge technologies, students develop problem-solving abilities and programming expertise. The department's commitment to staying at the forefront of technological advancements ensures graduates are well-prepared for dynamic careers in software development, data analysis, and emerging fields, contributing to the ever-evolving landscape of computer science.
